They found that these electrical synapses contain multiple gap junctions and components of adherens junctions, suggesting a close relationship between these structures. This research provides insights into the structural organization and functional diversity of electrical synapses in animals, shedding light on how these intercellular structures work together to facilitate communication in the nervous system.
